# OnDB

**We enable intelligence for AI Agents by giving them access to private and proprietary data.**

OnDB helps SaaS platforms and data providers monetize their proprietary data for AI agents -- with automatic per-query billing, discovery, and zero migration. 80% of the world's data is private. AI agents need it to be useful, and they're willing to pay for it. OnDB is the economic layer that turns bot traffic from a cost center into a revenue stream.

## How It Works

**Connect** -- Wrap any HTTP API, REST endpoint, or database with our open-source connectors. No data migration required.

**Discover** -- AI agents find your data automatically through standardized [skills.md](https://docs.ondb.ai) definitions.

**Monetize** -- Earn revenue from every AI query. Automatic per-query payments via [x402](https://www.x402.org/) and Stripe's [Machine Payments Protocol (MPP)](https://stripe.com/blog/machine-payments-protocol).

**Join** -- Cross-provider data joins with automatic revenue splitting between providers.
